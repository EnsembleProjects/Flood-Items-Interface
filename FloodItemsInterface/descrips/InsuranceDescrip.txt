The insurance policy document is very 
important so keep it safe & dry.

Make a note of the insurance incident 
line and your policy number. 
Keep in a purse/wallet in case you 
have to leave without belongings. 

Alternatively store a photo of details.

Remember: Digital information may be 
inaccessible in a power cut or if 
internet service is disrupted. 
Electronic devices can be destroyed 
by water so keep a backup.
